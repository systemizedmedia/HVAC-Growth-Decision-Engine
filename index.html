Math-based growth diagnostics

HVAC Growth Decision Engine

Paste or upload channel/campaign data to identify bottlenecks, MER, funnel trends, and budget allocation opportunities without arbitrary weighted scores.

Core rule

No made-up scores. Only actual inputs, ratios, trend comparisons, and scenario math.

1. Add Data

Required columns: date, channel, campaign, spend, leads, appointments, sales, revenue.

Dates should be in YYYY-MM-DD format. Revenue is optional, but MER needs revenue.

3. Bottleneck Detection

Uses actual current funnel vs. selected baseline. The bottleneck is the stage with the largest mathematically modeled lost sales if restored to baseline.

4. Budget Allocation Ranking

Ranks where additional budget would be associated with the most sales/revenue if each channel or campaign maintained its actual efficiency in the selected window.

5. Channel Analysis

Actual performance by channel for the selected timeframe.

6. Campaign Analysis

Actual performance by campaign for the selected timeframe.

7. Timeframe Comparison

Compares MER, funnel rates, and volume across 7D, MTD, 30D, 90D, and 365D.
